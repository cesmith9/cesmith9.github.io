# Coachella Timeline 
### Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival is a two-weekend event held every April in Indio, California. During its first appearence in 1999, the festival was know as for its niche music festival has grown into a cultural phenomon, drawing hundreds of thousdands to the desert annually. Coachella is known for its star-studded lineup, iconic art, installations, and desert fashion. Many now refer to it as the "influencer Olympics," as social media personalities and celebrites flock to the event, often sponsored by major brands such as Revolve. Beyond the glitz, Coachellla also showcases emergign artists, sustainbaility initiative, and immersive art, making it a multifaceted expereince that extneds far beyond just the music.
